An Inventory Solution as Unique as You

Stash is an online inventory system designed for small at home maker's businesses and artists. A solution designed specifically for artists, crafter's, home mechanics, and the like, Stash provides three main solutions to home supply and product storage:

Customize

Want to know what you put in the second drawer of the blue dresser? Looking for the scraper for blue paint? Can't remember how many strands of pearls you have on hand? Stash allows you to customize all aspects of your items and locations- unique to your needs!

Plan

If you're life's passion is taking unrelated materials and bringing them together into something new Stash can help. Standard inventory systems don't allow for tracking the unique relationships between a completed item and the materials used to make it. Stash does. Can't remeber what you used to create that bird house? Stash allows you to look up the project and see all the materials you needed last time- and check if you have enough to get started today!

Conquer

Stash provides a clean, easy to use webdesign that anyone can master. Standard inventory systems can be complex and convoluted- hard for the average user to learn. Weather you're a web savvy Etsy business, a just starting pre-teen, or an experienced home mechanic who's a bit computer shy, Stash provides an easy to understand solution to all your storage and making needs.
